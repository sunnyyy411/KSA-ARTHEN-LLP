Soapstone (Talc)

Our premium quality soapstone (talc) is known for its whiteness, purity, and excellent physical properties. It is carefully mined and processed to meet various industrial specifications.

Applications

  • Plastics industry - as a filler and reinforcing agent
  • Paints and coatings - as an extender and filler
  • Ceramics - in manufacturing sanitaryware, tiles, and insulators
  • Paper industry - as a filler to improve smoothness and opacity
  • Cosmetics - in talcum powders and makeup products
  • Pharmaceuticals - as an inert filler in tablets

Grades Available

Industrial Grade

For general industrial applications

Premium Grade

For high-value applications requiring superior whiteness

Micronized

Ultra-fine grades for specialized applications

Magnesite

Our high-quality magnesite products are sourced from carefully selected deposits and processed to meet exacting industry standards for various applications.

Applications

  • Refractory industry - for manufacturing bricks and linings
  • Agriculture - as a magnesium supplement in fertilizers
  • Chemical industry - as a source of magnesium compounds
  • Environmental applications - for wastewater treatment
  • Animal feed - as a nutritional supplement
  • Construction - in special cement formulations

Raw Magnesite

Natural magnesite ore in various sizes

Calcined Magnesite

Dead burned and caustic calcined grades

Magnesium Oxide

Various purity levels for different applications
